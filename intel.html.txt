## 🌐 Deployment

Deploy instantly on:

- **Netlify** – drag & drop `index.html` onto [app.netlify.com/drop](https://app.netlify.com/drop)  
- **Vercel** – `vercel --prod` or drag to [vercel.com/new](https://vercel.com/new)  
- **GitHub Pages** – push to a repo and enable Pages
